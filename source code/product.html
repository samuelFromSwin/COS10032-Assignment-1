EZ-Accounting Service Range

EZ-Accounting is a simple online helper for small businesses. It uses basic artificial intelligence to help you keep track of money coming in and going out. Instead of typing every detail into a notebook or spreadsheet, the system can read receipts, organise them into clear groups and store them safely. This gives you more time to focus on your work instead of worrying about bookwork.

Let EZ-Accounting do the boring money jobs

  • Take photos of receipts and store them in one place.
  • Sort money in and out into simple, easy to read groups.
  • Show a clear list of recent money activity.
  • Make basic reports you can share with your bookkeeper or teacher.

Our Service Plans

EZ-Accounting has three main plans. Each plan suits a different type of customer. You can start with the smallest plan and switch to another plan later if your business grows or you need more features. Every plan includes secure online access and simple email help.

Basic Plan

The Basic plan is for people who are just starting a small business or side job. It helps you record simple money in and money out. You can see a list of what you have spent and what you have earned without using paper or a calculator.

Optional features for the Basic plan:

  • Let up to two people upload photos of receipts from their phones.
  • Add a simple tax summary for small Australian businesses.
  • Connect one small online shop, such as Etsy or Shopify.

Standard Plan

The Standard plan is for small but busy businesses. It is useful when you send and receive a lot of payments each week. The system helps you see who has paid, who still needs to pay, and where most of your money is going.

Optional features for the Standard plan:

  • Use a ready-made set of money categories for your type of business.
  • Turn on a simple approval step before large payments are made.
  • View basic summaries for more than one store or location at once.

Pro Plan

The Pro plan is for groups or teams that talk about money regularly, such as once a month in a meeting. It creates clear summary pages that show what has changed since last month so you can quickly see if things are getting better or worse.

Optional features for the Pro plan:

  • Create simple budgets and compare them with what really happened.
  • Automatically make monthly summary files you can print or email.
  • Get faster setup help and priority email support from EZ-Accounting staff.

How to Choose a Plan

If you are not sure which plan to pick, use these simple steps. They are written for people who may be new to business or accounting.

  1. Choose the Basic plan if you only need simple records.
  2. Choose the Standard plan if you send regular invoices.
  3. Choose the Pro plan if your team discusses money each month.

Getting Started with EZ-Accounting

Most new customers follow these easy steps:

  1. Create an online account for your business.
  2. Connect your bank or upload past money records.
  3. Check that the categories make sense for your work.
  4. Invite any staff who need access.
